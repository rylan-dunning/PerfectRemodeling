We've got what you need!

Every project, every customer is vital to the reputation of our business! We view every job as an opportunity to do our best work possible! Your experience with Perfect Remodeling, LLC will include top workmanship, prompt service, strong communication throughout the process, clean work practices, and efficient time utilization.

We specialize in remodeling of bathrooms, kitchens, patios, basements, and any other areas in your house. We also take on smaller scale projects as well! So, if that faucet is leaking, toilet acting up, water heater going out, or for any other minor repairs & upgrades, give us a call!

As general contractors, we perform all the tasks ourselves! We have full control of the project, turnkey, thus reducing the price and time of completion of the project! The initial consultation is free, and you will have a detailed quote to review (usually 3 to 5 days) after the consultation.

We take care of it all! Starting with design and spatial concepts, planning and coordination, demolition, removal, inspection, construction (if applicable), installation of tile, flooring, trim, lighting, electrical, plumbing, cosmetics, drywall, texturizing, paint, finishing touches, clean up.

At Your Service

BATHROOM

We install a wide variety of materials and equipment to make your bathroom space amazing! If you already know what you want, then great! If not, we can suggest pattern/ color schemes to fit your space and budget. A basic remodel of a typical bathroom takes us about 2 to 3 weeks. A custom bathroom remodel is 4 to 5 weeks.

KITCHEN

We will assist you with ideas on bringing your kitchen workspace to life! The kitchen is a versatile space that serves as a meal preparation station, as well as a gathering place for friends and family. The kitchen should practical, yet warm and inviting. We will perform the renovations on your kitchen with speed and accuracy. We know that nobody wants to live in a construction zone for 12 months, so we don’t bounce around to different job sites. We stay focused on your project until completion. Kitchen renovations take us 3 to 4 weeks for a standard package. Custom/ extensive kitchen remodeling will take us 6 to 8 weeks, or more, depending on the complexity of the project.

OUTDOOR PATIO / DECK

Like most people, we love the great outdoors! We will repair, rebuild, or make a brand-new structure to suit your needs. We build patio covers and decks with a variety of motifs and styles to fit your home. Whether it’s cedar, redwood, composite decking-building materials, or stone hardscapes, we are well versed in pulling it all together to make the outdoor space of your dreams.

FIREPLACE

Want to give your fireplace a fresh new look, from hearth to mantle? We can restore your fire feature with exciting materials to match your taste and compliment the surrounding spaces. We also convert fireplace natural wood burning fireboxes to gas log burning sets for on-demand ambience!

STAIRCASES AND HANDRAILS

One of the most powerful ways to give your home that WOW factor, is to convert your carpeted stairs, to hard materials. Traditional hard woods are timeless, beautiful, and offer a wide variety of options. In recent years, composite materials are an increasingly popular option, due to their ding, scratch resistance, durability, and realistic appearance. Also known as engineered flooring, these materials can be used to renovate your staircase to give it more contemporary and attractive look. We also install handrails and related components to match.

MISCELLANEOUS REPAIRS / UPGRADES

Day to day life sometimes brings unplanned disruptions. When your sink, toilet, water heater, lighting fixture, ceiling fan, light switch, handrail, drywall repair, just to name a few, goes south, give us a call, and we’ll respond quickly! Give us your list of things to get done around the house, and we’ll get those taken care of!
